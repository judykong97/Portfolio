About Me

I'm Junhan (Judy) Kong, a master's student in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University.

I started my research in human-computer interaction and accessibility when I was an undergrad at CMU. My research focuses on creating intelligent, scalable, and accessible systems for people with disabilities.

I'm currently working towards my master's research thesis and I'm advised by Prof. Jeffrey Bigham.
